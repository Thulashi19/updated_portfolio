Heart Beat

Heart Beat is a user-friendly, responsive website designed to provide essential health services and resources. It serves as a one-stop solution for individuals seeking medical care, information about heart health, and access to various health services.

Features

  • 1)🔍 Navigation & Accessibility – Quick access to About, Services, and Contact.
  • 2)🎯 Hero Section – Clear core message with service and contact links.
  • 3)❤️ About Section – Highlights Heart Beat’s mission and patient care focus.
  • 4)📊 Health Stats – Displays patients served, consultations, and success rates.
  • 5)✉️ Contact Form – Simple form for inquiries and consultations.
  • 6)📞 Contact Info – Phone, address, and business hours for easy reach.
