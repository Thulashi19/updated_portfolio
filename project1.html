Heart Beat

Heart Beat is a clean, responsive web app offering essential heart health resources and a smooth user experience.

Project Features

  • Navigation & Accessibility – Smooth scroll to About, Services, and Contact.
  • Hero Section – Clear, engaging intro with action buttons.
  • About Section – Highlights mission and patient-focused approach.
  • Health Stats – Showcases user metrics and service success.
  • Contact Form – Easy-to-use inquiry submission.
  • Quick Contact Info – Includes phone, address, and hours.
